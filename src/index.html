URL Shortener takes your long URLs and converts them into short, manageable links. When someone clicks your short link, they're instantly redirected to the original URL. We track all clicks and provide detailed analytics about your link's performance.

Our free plan allows you to create up to 100 links per month with basic analytics. You get 30 days of click history and standard support. It's perfect for personal use, testing, or low-volume needs.

Yes! Custom domains are available on our Pro and Business plans. You can connect your own domain to create branded short links that match your website. The Pro plan includes 1 custom domain, while Business includes 5.
